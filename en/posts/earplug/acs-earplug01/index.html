ACS Custom Noise Cancelling Earplugs

I decided to share my experience since there aren’t many people talking about this on internet.

So why did I want custom earplugs? Mainly because my friends often invite me to hardstyle nightclubs and music festivals like Defqon.1, Intents, etc., and eardrums are very fragile. I don’t want to lose my hearing so early QQ

But there’s another reason, which might be the bigger one, that sometimes I need to work on equipment in the network room or server room, and sometimes I stay there for a long time, and the high-frequency fans are super noisy… For these reasons, I wanted to find a way to protect my ears.

Actually, I didn’t need to go for custom ones, I could just use off-the-shelf products, but since I was going to spend money anyway, I thought it would be nice to try custom ones, so I’ll share how I bought them.

Choosing the Style

First, I found the official website, this link takes you directly to the custom Pro 17, because that’s the base model I chose.

I chose the full-concha style because the description said it wouldn’t be affected by wearing a helmet, though I don’t know how I would need that XD. I recommend the handle style because it’s easier to put on and take off, the full-concha style is a bit inconvenient in that regard.

Next, I selected the Taipei ACS for earmould making and added it to the cart.

I also recommend adding the neck cord, it’s really convenient. After taking them off, you can just let them hang around your neck without worrying about losing them. The neck cord is quite sturdy, it won’t easily fall off even if you accidentally pull on it.

The remaining personalized options like color or engraving are up to you if you want to add them. The order will first go to Taipei ACS, so if you need to add anything else, you can do so when you’re getting your earmould made.

After choosing, you can check out on the website’s shopping cart page. I used LinePay, yay for e-payments!

Book for Earmould Making

After placing the order, I messaged ACS’s official Facebook account, you can also call them.

The whole process takes less than 30 minutes if it goes smoothly. They’ll first explain and introduce things to you, then they start filling your ears with earmold material?!

The feeling during the process is quite cool, it’s cold, and then after a few seconds, the filling starts to expand, filling up your ear completely (,,・ω・,,)

After about 5 minutes, it dries and they take it out, it looks something like this

Actually, after this step, I added color to the earplugs XD. I originally chose transparent, but the staff said they might turn yellow over time since it’s plastic (they let me change the color for free XD). They have samples of all the colors on-site, there are translucent ones, ones with glitter added, etc.

Finally, after confirming all the order details with the staff, they send it to the UK for production, and then you wait 4 ~ 5 weeks for it to be delivered.

Unboxing

I received them after about 4 weeks. Here’s what’s included:

The metal tin on the right is small and cute, about the size of a round compact powder case XD. I also added pro 26 filters because I saw on Reddit that someone said Defqon.1 can get as loud as 100+ decibels, so I was scared and bought these. The server room I work in is also noisy, so buying a higher filter level is fine. I didn’t use the lubricant at all, I just started trying to insert them at different angles as soon as I got them, luckily it didn’t hurt.

The transparent ones look pretty good, there’s a picture on ACS’s Facebook that looks even better QQ

The inside of the earplugs has red for right and blue for left markings by default, the outside is where the customization is.

According to the staff, if the earplugs are well maintained, they can last for 4 ~ 5 years, which is quite long, I might lose them before then XD

Experience

The most important thing is definitely how they feel when worn. I had a few friends try them on ლ(◉◞౪◟◉ )ლ

Everyone said it was amazing! With both pro 26 and pro 17, the voices you hear are almost as clear as normal speaking voices, the volume is only slightly reduced, it’s amazing!!!

I also tested them in the server room, which is about as loud as 50 Dyson hairdryers running at full speed within 1 meter, with that kind of high-frequency noise. The effect of using pro 26 and pro 17 was equally significant, the noise was directly transformed into a gentle hairdryer sound, I even felt sleepy XD. And they are indeed comfortable for long wear, I wore them continuously for 2 ~ 3 hours without feeling tired.

One thing to mention is that I wore them to the main stage GA area at S2O, and honestly, after the filters got sprayed with water, the sound became very muffled. I was getting sprayed with water guns from the moment I entered, and even in the VIP area, the water was blown into the rest area by the wind, so the earplugs actually caused a bit of trouble QQ ( fortunately, the main stage at S2O wasn’t too loud this time, so it was okay to listen to one set). Oh right! Wearing earplugs at the main stage or side VIP seats actually filters out some high-frequency noise, making the melody of the music clearer and less harsh.

That’s about it for custom earplugs. They’re a bit expensive, but damaging your eardrums is not good, so it’s worth the money!

This time I spent about 10,000 NTD because I added filters * 2, color * 2, these two are calculated separately for each ear ~
